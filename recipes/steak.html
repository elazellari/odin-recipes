Ribeye Steak

Description

This is a Ribeye Steak recipe, where I will describe how you can cook the perfect steak

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Olive Oil
  • Kosher Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Thyme or Rosemary
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat Olive Oil in the pan with medium to high heat
  2. Sear Steak both sides for 1 min each
  3. Lower down temperature to medium and add butter
  4. Turn over steak every 30 second while basting it
