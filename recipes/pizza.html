Pizza

Description

This is a pizza recipe, where I will describe how you can cook the perfect pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour 00
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Bacon
  • Mushrooms
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare dough 8 hours before making pizza
  2. Add tomatoe sauce
  3. Add bacon, mushrooms and mozzarella cheese
  4. Bake in oven for 50 minutes
